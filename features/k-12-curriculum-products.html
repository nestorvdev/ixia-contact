K-12 Curriculum Products

The rise of apps and educational games is creating new opportunities for schools.

Schools in the US are adopting technology faster than ever before, not just for content dissemination but also for managing content and student data. The need for accountability to key stakeholders – parents, students and state bodies - is higher than ever before. At the same time, the explosive growth of social media and the proliferation of new, smaller devices is providing newer, better ways for students to learn. There is an increasing need for students to be career ready and college ready. The rise of apps and educational games is creating new opportunities for schools. And, of course, big data and associated analytics are enabling schools to create smarter, more focussed learning.

At Ixia, we offer the full gamut of services to our clients – both publishers and educational institutions – to make learning richer, more effective and more contemporary.

These include design and development of courseware aligned to existing de facto standards (including the Common Core), design of standardized assessments, test packs, responsive design, rich media interactivity, design and deployment of apps across various platforms, quality assurance and testing (for both content and technology solutions), cloud computing and storage, and a library of CTE electives to help make students college and career ready.

We also maintain and deploy student data to enable clients to perform analytics on the same.
