About Us

We are an education technology company. We research, design, develop, localize, and process content. We also architect, develop, maintain, and test software applications that manage content, students and teachers, and academic operations of schools and colleges. Ixia also offers solutions in data analytics.

Ixia was founded with the vision to partner with education technology and content providers to develop standards-aligned, mobile-optimized learning products within budget and in the shortest time to market. We share our customers’ product vision from ideation through launch and beyond.

At the core of the company’s rich pedigree in the education space is the leadership team’s significant experience since 1998 in providing multi-shore learning product development services to some of the world’s largest education publishers and software vendors.
